I am a PhD candidate in the CREATE Lab, Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. My recent research focuses on the design, implementation, and evaluation of civic technologies: information technology for public good. I develop interactive systems and applications which integrate artificial intelligence, crowdsourcing, and data visualization techniques to support citizen empowerment.

Research Interests

Civic Technology / Citizen Science
Human Computation and Crowdsourcing
Interactive Data Visualization and Storytelling
Machine Learning / Computer Vision
